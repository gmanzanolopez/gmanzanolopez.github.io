ABOUT ME

Biography

I was born on August, 22 1998 in El Salvador, Central America. I came with my parents to the U.S when I was 5 years old, we lived in Washinton D.C, for about 6 months, then moved to Palmdale, California for about a year. After that we ended up in San Antonio, TX, and have been here for the past 14 years.

I had my first child at the age of 17, and got married at 19. I was able to buy my first home just about 8 months ago, and my ultimate goal in life is to own a family business and build my own house.
